  • It is best to start the round with either nj.rh or walk forward one frame and c.rh or c.fwd
  • On wakeup, follow up meaty c.fwd with either c.rh, tick throw, or doing nothing to bait DP
  • On wakeup, follow up meaty cs.str with c.str tick throw, c.rh, or doing nothing to bait DP
  • If he walks backwards, keep walking him to the corner until he actually does something
  • Make him whiff tiger shots by neutral jumping lows, ducking highs
  • You can whiff punish most all of his buttons by standing outside of c.fwd range
  • Walk forward if he walks forward or simply stops throwing fireballs when you neutral jump one
  • After a throw, if you do fj.sh it will beat him if he jumps back to evade, and will whiff DP when you cross over
  • You can only forward jump punish Tiger Shots near full-screen or at neutral
  • Punish blocked close range DPs with throw
  • Punish blocked far range DPs by walking closer and hitting s.fwd
  • Do not push buttons like c.rh to push yourself out of range once you get proper spacing
  • Lock him down in the corner, prevent him from building meter.
  • Is most likely to do super on wakeup if trapped in the corner, be prepared for this by either jumping or blocking outside of throw range.
  • Don't throw fireballs in neutral, he has too many options to evade and punish.
  • Use neckbreaker and cs.str in the corner to mix-up pressure.
  • If he techs a throw in the corner, follow up with c.rh or nj.rh. Attempting to set-up the throw again immediately tends to get beaten by reversal throw.
  • Don't jump out after you block s.fwd or s.rh, you will typically get met in the air by double j.str.
  • You can beat headpress with nj.sht
  • j.fwd beats Crusher.
